Our Services

Gas Delivery and Distribution

Natural Gas Supply: Providing a reliable and consistent supply of natural gas to customers.
Pipeline Network: Maintaining and operating a vast network of underground and above-ground pipelines to deliver gas to customers.
Meter Installation and Reading: Installing gas meters at customer premises and regularly reading them to accurately bill for consumption.

Customer Service and Billing

Account Management: Assisting customers with account setup, changes, and inquiries.
Billing and Payment: Issuing accurate bills and providing various payment options.
Emergency Response: Handling gas leaks, outages, and other emergencies promptly.

Safety and Compliance

Safety Regulations: Adhering to safety standards and regulations to ensure the safe delivery and use of gas.
Leak Detection: Implementing systems to detect and repair gas leaks promptly.
Emergency Preparedness: Developing and practicing emergency response plans.

Infrastructure Maintenance and Upgrades

Pipeline Maintenance: Regularly inspecting, repairing, and upgrading gas pipelines to ensure their reliability and safety.
Equipment Maintenance: Maintaining gas regulators, valves, and other equipment.
Infrastructure Expansion: Expanding the gas network to accommodate growth in demand.

Environmental Stewardship

Emissions Reduction: Implementing measures to reduce greenhouse gas emissions associated with gas production and distribution.
Energy Efficiency: Promoting energy-efficient gas appliances and usage practices.
Environmental Compliance: Adhering to environmental regulations and standards.
